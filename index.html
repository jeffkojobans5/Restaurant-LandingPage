Restaurant Story


La Tante bc10 Restaurant, is a public private partnership restaurant established in Accra which operates from the defunct Ghana Airways bc-10 carrier. It is the first aeroplane restaurant to be established in Africa.It is opposite the Marina Mall at the Airport City in Accra.

The La Tante bc 10 restaurant is fully air-conditioned and has a seating capacity of 118. The chairs and tables have been re-designed to meet high operational standards with separate wash rooms for male and female customers.
